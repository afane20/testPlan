Who am I?

I'm not your typical college student, actually far from it. Here is a little bit about me that you may not know. I've been married for almost 6 years, and have 3 children: 2 boys and a girl (see picture to the below). I've worked in the computer/IT industry for over 7 years before I was inspired to come back to school full-time to get my degree. This is my second semester here at BYU-Idaho and I have loved every minute of it! Just before transfering here to BYU-I I was working for a investment managment firm in Seattle doing in house application development using VB and C# .NET. All of the software developed were windows-based application and it has been quite some time since I have done any web-based development. I have however have had some experience with ASP.NET and AJAX.

Other then going to school, work, and spending time with the family some of the things I enjoy doing are: playing sports (mostly baseball, football, and volleyball), camping, fishing, fixing cars and other things, spending time on the computer, and playing the Wii.
